Why them?

YOUR UNION, In partnership with other local area unions, has united as the Wichita / Hutchinson Labor Federation. Our goal was to survey, interview, and endorse the best candidates for local office – those who will champion the rights of working families in Wichita.

Endorsed candidates have pledged their commitment to:

  • Ensuring public safety services are of top quality, including road maintenance.
  • Advocating for higher wages and job safety protections.
  • Guaranteeing every Kansan access to a high-quality public education system.
  • Upholding the dignity and respect of all workers including the right to unionize.
